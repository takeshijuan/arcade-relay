# Maintainers

ArcadeRelay is currently maintained by:

- `@takeshijuan` - project owner and primary maintainer

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Keep the harness contract coherent.
- Review changes to `.claude/docs/contract.md`, skills, workflows, and agents.
- Triage issues and security reports.
- Decide release readiness and publish release notes.
- Ensure generated asset and license guidance stays accurate.

## Becoming a Maintainer

The project is currently single-maintainer. Regular contributors may be invited
to maintain specific areas after sustained, high-quality contributions and
review judgment in that area.
